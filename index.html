About Us

We realize that deciding who to hire to work in your home is an important decision. We, at Last Detail take pride in our work and will treat you and your home with the utmost respect. This begins with returning phone calls promptly, continues with being punctual to appointments, listening to and meeting your needs, supplying a detailed proposal with honest and accurate information and concludes with leaving the job site "broom swept clean". We set our standards higher than other contractors because we know how difficult it can be to find an honest, experienced, respectful, efficient contractor. Our goal is to exceed your expectations...to make sure you are satisfied with every "Last Detail"!!!


Last Detail has successfully completed projects in all aspects of general contracting, pleasing our customers all along the way for more than 16 years. Additions, bathrooms, kitchen remodels, basements, etc...all of our projects are important to us. Our experienced team of masons, framers, plumbers, electricians, tile workers, painters and carpenters provides you with all the services needed to complete any and all home remodeling.


In addition, we insure our employees with State Workers' Insurance and liability insurance thru State Farm Insurance Company. This not only protects our employees, it protects YOU.


Over the years we have formed bonds with many of our customers and take pride in knowing that they call LAST DETAIL for all of their remodeling needs. Have you heard the saying "when you find a good contractor, you keep him? Well, Last Detail is the company you are looking to keep, the company you will be proud you chose. Just ask our many repeat customers.
